The Illustrious XL & NoobAI-XL Style Explorer is a comprehensive visual library and prompt reference tool designed to help artists and prompt engineers master Danbooru-style artist tags in the two leading anime-focused SDXL models: the massively popular Illustrious XL and its high-performance fine-tune NoobAI-XL. Whether you generate in ComfyUI, Automatic1111, or Forge, this explorer gives you instant visual previews, dataset insights, and and one-click optimized artist tags that work seamlessly in both models.

The Power of Illustrious XL and NoobAI-XL

Illustrious XL (by OnomaAI Research) is one of the most widely used and trusted anime illustration models in the Stable Diffusion community. Built on a strong Kohaku XL foundation and trained on vast Danbooru datasets, it delivers exceptional tag adherence, vibrant colors, and professional-grade detail — which is why it serves as the base for hundreds of popular merges and LoRAs.

NoobAI-XL (NAI-XL) by Laxhar Lab takes this foundation even further. It is a state-of-the-art fine-tune built directly on Illustrious XL, retrained on approximately 12.7 million images from the latest complete Danbooru and e621 datasets. This results in superior character consistency, even more precise Danbooru tag understanding, and noticeably better lighting, shadows, and color fidelity thanks to its advanced V-Prediction architecture.

Because NoobAI-XL is a direct descendant of Illustrious XL, every artist style in this explorer works perfectly in both models. What you see here is 100% compatible — giving you maximum flexibility no matter which model you prefer.

  • Illustrious XL Strengths: Exceptional popularity, broad community support, excellent tag prompting, and native high-resolution capabilities up to 1536×1536.
  • V-Prediction Architecture (NoobAI-XL): Superior color gamut, richer light and shadow rendering, and significantly fewer artifacts compared to standard epsilon-prediction models.
  • Massive Shared Dataset: Both models draw from millions of Danbooru-tagged images, ensuring unparalleled understanding of artist styles, character details, and quality boosters like masterpiece, best quality, and newest.
  • High-Resolution Native Training: Optimized for crisp professional results across common aspect ratios (768x1344, 832x1216, 1152x896, and more).

Optimal Generation Settings for Both Models

These recommended settings deliver the best results in NoobAI-XL and also perform excellently in the base Illustrious XL (or any of its merges). Fine-tune them slightly based on your specific workflow.

  • Sampling Method: Euler A (strongly recommended for both models; other samplers can introduce unwanted artifacts).
  • CFG Scale: 4.5 – 5.0 (V-Prediction in NoobAI-XL shines at lower guidance; Illustrious XL also benefits from reduced CFG compared to older SDXL models).
  • Steps: 28 – 35 steps (excellent balance of quality and speed).
  • Prompting Style: Comma-separated normalized Danbooru tags work best. Avoid long natural-language sentences for maximum adherence in both models.
